Baked Ricotta, Tomato & Chickpea Pasta

Description:

Inspired by the Baked Feta Pasta trend this recipe is simple and easy and will be a new mid-week favourite! It’s packed full of protein thanks to our Pasta Pro so it’s a great vegetarian friendly dinner idea!

Ingredients:

  • San Remo Pasta Pro Penne 250g
  • 200g tinned chickpeas
  • 1 punnet baby roma tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 garlic clove, thinly sliced
  • 100g firm ricotta, crumbled
  • 30g mixed pitted olives
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Sea salt & cracked black pepper
  • 2 tbsp chopped parsley
  • 2 tbsp grated parmesan

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 190°C.
  2. Place chickpeas, tomatoes, onion, garlic, ricotta and olives in an oven tray.
  3. Drizzle with olive oil, season with salt & pepper and bake for 10 minutes.
  4. Meanwhile, in a pot of boiling salted water, cook pasta as per packet instructions.
  5. Add cooked pasta to the oven tray and mix through. Garnish with parsley and parmesan.
  6. Serve and enjoy with family and friends!
